META Formation

META is formed with the primary objective of exposing the
aspiring mechanical engineers to the practical aspects and
professional challenges for their sustainable growth.

META was formally inaugurated by our Director
(Incharge), Dr. Saidi Reddy on 8th November 2019 in the
presence of Dr. C. Vyjayanthi, HoD Mechanical and was
attended by the faculty, staff and students of Mechanical
and Civil Engineering. The inaugural ceremony included
Logo launch, highlighting the activities conducted and
future plans.
